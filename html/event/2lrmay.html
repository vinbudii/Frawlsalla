HEATWAVE 2021 SUMMER EVENT IS HERE!

BY BLUE MAMMOTH | JUN 2, 2021 | PATCH NOTES, WEEKLY ROTATION

TIt’s hero time! Power up your Omnitrix for this week’s Brawl of the Week, Morph. Choose one weapon each from 3 of your favorite Legends in character select and use the throw button to change forms. First to 3 KOs wins!

Get out the sun block, because Valhalla’s hottest seasonal event returns next week! Heatwave 2021 begins on Wednesday, June 2nd.

To catch the latest Brawlhalla news and content, be sure to follow us on Twitter, Twitch, FaceBook, YouTube, Instagram and Discord!

Legend Rotation

The free-to-play Legend rotation for this week includes: Jiro, Caspian, Xull, Val, Diana, Thor, Brynn, Magyar, and Artemis.

  • Barraza – A feared man in the wastelands with his Blasters and Axe, Barraza is the ultimate survivor and will do what it takes to be at the top in Valhalla.
  • Scarlet – A former female boxing champion of England and head of the Royal Airship Navy, this Legend uses her ingenuity and steam power to build her own Rocket Lance and Hammer to take into battle.
  • Wu Shang – Using the way of peace, discipline and martial arts, he takes down his foes with Spear and Gauntlets!
  • Fait – Speaking to the stars and using glimpses of the future, Fait battles for good with her Scythe and Orb!
