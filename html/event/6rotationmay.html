HEATWAVE 2021 SUMMER EVENT IS HERE!

BY BLUE MAMMOTH | JUN 2, 2021 | PATCH NOTES, WEEKLY ROTATION

Outlaws of the galaxy beware! Reno, the Chitnoid Bounty Hunter makes his free-to-play Legend Rotation debut today. That means that you can now play as everyone’s favorite alien bug cowboy for free from now until May 18th.

This week’s Brawl of the Week is Brawlball Bash! Team up with a couple of friends or leave it to the matchmaking queue. Then work together to score 3 goals before your opponents do.

It’s the last week of Bloomhalla, so don’t forget to check out the garden of goodies available in Mallhalla. Although this year’s event is wilting away, Bloomhalla will return brighter than ever for springtime next year with a harvest of new Seasonal items!

It’s also the last week for our Jötunn Winter Battle Pass. For those looking to receive the exclusive Jötunn Royalty Avatar, complete all the missions before the season ends on May 18th!

To catch the latest Brawlhalla news and content, be sure to follow us on Twitter, Twitch, FaceBook, YouTube, Instagram and Discord!

Legend Rotation

This week Reno blasts his way into the free-to-play Legend rotation! Also featuring: Kor, Sir Roland, Rayman, Magyar, Dusk, Sidra, Ember, and Nix.

  • Reno – Four arms, compound eyes, fearlessness, and an added dose of genius make this Chitnoid gunslinger the greatest bounty hunter this side of the Pecosid asteroid belt. He’ll take on any job with his trusty Blasters and loyal companion, Orbot.
  • Kor – Blackguard Keep’s left platform mover takes his place in the arena wielding both Gauntlets and Hammer.
  • Sir Roland – No one in Valhalla feels a greater thirst for victory in the tournament than Sir Roland, as he strikes down his opponents with Rocket Lance and Sword.
  • Rayman -The Champion of the Glade of Dreams, wielding his Gauntlets and Axe, has found his way into Valhalla on a mission to save his home.
  • Magyar – As the last remaining member of the Batavian Strazci army and wielding the strength of a 100 elite knights, this spectral guardian has come for the glory of battle with their Greatsword and Hammer!
  • Dusk – Wielding his Spear and a powerful Orb, carved from stone and magic, Dusk seeks to spread chaos throughout Valhalla.
  • Sidra – The Corsair Queen, known for her legendary and fearless raids, is prepared to fight with her Sword and Cannon!
  • Ember – This quick and dexterous elf does battle in Valhalla with Bow, Katars and her two companions: a wolf named Ash and her raven named Yarra.
  • Nix – Led by a thousand year hunt, this reaper, armed with both Blasters and Scythe, has found herself a new bounty in Valhalla.
