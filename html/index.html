What Is Frawlsalla?

Frawlsalla is a free-to-play 2D fighting game where you can Play locally with friends, fight in chaotic Free-For-All matches online and offline, team up for 2v2 battles.

If you’re looking to unwind with friends, or if you’re aiming to carve your name into the pillars of the Frawlsalla Power Rankings, you can play Frawlsalla however and whenever you want.

Choose A Legend

Over 40 Legends to chose from, each with unique movesets, signature abilities, stats, weapon combinations and flair to suit your personal playstyle. Master one, or learn them all.

More legends continue to join the fight in an ever-expanding roster of those who fight for glory, honor, and bragging rights in the Brawlhalla arenas.
